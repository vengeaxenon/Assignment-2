General Users

The Athletes' Gym was initially opened to help facilitate the training regimes of NTU
varsity athletes. However, the Athletes' Gym is now officially extended to the general
student population of NTU.

For general users who wish to access the gym, you can register here. Do note that it is
mandatory to attend a gym orientation before any usage of the gym. There will be a first
time registration fee of $10.00, as well as an annual renewal fee of $5.00.
